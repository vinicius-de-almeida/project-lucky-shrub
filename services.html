Our Services

At Lucky Shrub, we are your one-stop shop to "transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of". We offer a complete range of services to bring your garden dreams to life.

Garden Design

Our expert team collaborates with you to create a beautiful garden design that reflects your personal style. We handle everything from the initial concept to the final blueprint, crafting a beautiful and functional green space you'll love for years to come.

Plant Nursery

Visit our nursery to find a wide variety of high-quality, healthy plants, specially selected to thrive in the local climate. Our knowledgeable staff will help you find the perfect flowers, shrubs, and trees to bring your garden to life.

Maintenance

Keep your garden looking its best year-round with our professional maintenance services. We manage everything from pruning and weeding to fertilization, ensuring your landscape remains healthy and vibrant so you can simply relax and enjoy it.
